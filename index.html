// how it works

Private by construction,
not by policy

01Oneiro runs powerful AI language models directly on your iPhone using Apple Silicon. Every analysis of your dreams happens on your chip — no network call, no cloud API, no third party in the loop.

◈
ON-DEVICE INFERENCE
The models run via Apple's MLX framework on the A17 Pro chip or newer. The model never touches the internet after download.
◈
DREAM MEMORY
01Oneiro builds a private catalog of your dreams over time, finding patterns and recurring symbols. Stored locally.
◈
NO ACCOUNT, NO LOGIN
Nothing to sign up for. No email, no password, no profile synced anywhere. Open the app and start writing.
◈
HONEST INTERPRETATIONS
The agent provides analytical, Jungian, or Freudian interpretations without cloud-based content censorship.
◈
COMPLETE CONTROL
Clear your dream journal or delete the app — all instant, all permanent.
◈
OFFLINE BY DEFAULT
After the one-time model download (~1–2 GB), 01Oneiro works with no internet connection at all.
