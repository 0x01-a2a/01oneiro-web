How it works

Record, interpret,
understand

Dreams are most vivid in the minutes after waking. Oneiro captures them before they fade and gives you meaning before you leave the bed.

01

Record on waking

Write or speak your dream the moment you wake. Oneiro captures every detail — symbols, emotions, settings — before they dissolve.

02

AI interprets

The on-device model applies Jungian, Freudian, or analytical frameworks — whichever fits — and surfaces what your subconscious might be processing.

03

Patterns emerge

Over time, Oneiro tracks recurring symbols, characters, and themes across your dream history. What repeats is what matters.

Features

Honest interpretations,
no censorship

Dreams can be dark, confusing, or intimate. Oneiro doesn't filter. It interprets what's actually there.

🧠

On-device AI model

Apple's MLX framework runs the model on your A17 Pro chip or newer. Dream analysis happens on your silicon — no network call, no cloud API.

🌀

Dream memory

Oneiro builds a private catalog of your dreams over time — recurring symbols, characters, locations — and identifies patterns across weeks and months.

🔭

Multiple frameworks

Choose Jungian archetypes, Freudian analysis, or plain narrative interpretation. The AI applies the framework you prefer to every dream.

✈

Works offline anywhere

After the one-time model download (~1–2 GB), Oneiro works with zero internet. Your most private moments don't need a connection.
